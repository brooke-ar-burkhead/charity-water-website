Our Mission

At charity:water, we believe everyone deserves access to clean and safe water. Our mission is to bring clean water to people in need by funding sustainable water projects, empowering local communities, and providing transparent updates every step of the way.

Clean Water Wells

We build sustainable wells in communities that need them most, providing access to clean, safe drinking water for generations.

Project Transparency

Track every project with GPS and photo updates, so you see exactly where your donation goes and the lives it transforms.

Community Education

We empower local leaders and teach communities about hygiene and maintenance to ensure long-term impact and health.

100% Model

100% of public donations fund clean water projects. Private donors cover our operating costs so every penny you give brings water to those in need.
